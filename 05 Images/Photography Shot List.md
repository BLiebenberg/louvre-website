# Photography Shot List

## Purpose
Create a reusable image bank for website, Google, OTAs, social, emailers, and brochures.

## Must-Have Shots
- Exterior entrance and signage.
- Reception or welcome moment.
- Best room angles.
- Beds with clean linen.
- Bathrooms.
- Breakfast and hot food.
- Dinner and catering.
- Guesthouse garden or outdoor spaces.
- Manor House garden and rooms.
- Gallery set for dining.
- Gallery set auditorium-style.
- People enjoying the venue.
- Secure parking and access points.

## Website Priority
- Homepage hero.
- Accommodation cards.
- Gallery section.
- Work Well section.
- Contact and map support images.

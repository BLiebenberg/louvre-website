# Breakfast Photography

## Purpose
Show food as practical, generous, and trustworthy.

## Shot Types
- Hot breakfast plate.
- Buffet or serving setup.
- Coffee and juice.
- Table setting.
- Guests eating if natural and permitted.
- Catering detail for functions.

## Style
- Warm, clean, appetising.
- Not too close or messy.
- Avoid empty or half-finished plates.
- Use natural serving context.

## Website Uses
- Accommodation + Catering card.
- Work Well page.
- Gallery page.
- Google updates.

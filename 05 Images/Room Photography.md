# Room Photography

## Purpose
Create room images that sell comfort and trust.

## Shot Types
- Wide room angle from doorway or corner.
- Bed straight-on.
- Bed detail.
- Desk or work area.
- Bathroom.
- Tea and coffee station.
- Wardrobe or storage.
- Aircon or comfort feature.

## Requirements
- Clean bedding.
- Lamps on.
- Curtains and blinds consistent.
- Remove clutter.
- Check mirrors.
- Straighten verticals.

## Crop Needs
- Website card crop.
- OTA landscape crop.
- Google square crop.
- Social vertical crop.

## Next Actions
- Build a room-by-room checklist.
- Identify weakest rooms needing a reshoot.

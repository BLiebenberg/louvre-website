# Image Style Guide

## Purpose
Keep Louvre imagery consistent, calm, and credible.

## Style
- Real rooms and real spaces.
- Natural light where possible.
- Warm but not yellow.
- Clean verticals.
- Straight lines.
- No over-editing.
- No heavy filters.

## Composition
- Use wider shots for room context.
- Use close-ups for detail and texture.
- Keep beds, tables, and venues tidy.
- Show people naturally, not staged awkwardly.

## Editing
- Correct exposure.
- Straighten verticals.
- Crop consistently.
- Keep colours true.
- Avoid excessive contrast or saturation.

## Reuse Rules
One good shoot should feed website, Google, OTAs, social, brochures, and emailers.

# Booking Flow

## Purpose
Make booking effortless and reduce confusion between properties.

## Primary Booking Paths
- Book Louvre Guesthouse.
- Book Louvre Manor House.
- Ask for a quote.
- Call or WhatsApp reception.
- Email reception.

## Recommended Flow
1. Guest lands on page.
2. Guest identifies stay type: Guesthouse, Manor, Little House, Gallery, Long Stay, NAMPO.
3. Guest clicks the correct booking or enquiry action.
4. Guest receives clear contact or booking confirmation.

## Quote-Based Flows
Use quote enquiry for:
- Long stays.
- Groups.
- Functions.
- Catering packages.
- Agent or company bookings.
- Uncertain property fit.

## Friction to Remove
- Conflicting check-in or check-out times.
- Duplicate or outdated addresses.
- Store-style cart/account links.
- Hidden booking buttons.
- Too many booking choices without explanation.

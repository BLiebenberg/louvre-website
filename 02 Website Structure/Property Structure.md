# Property Structure

## Purpose
Explain how each Louvre property fits into the wider hospitality collection.

## Louvre Guesthouse
Main hub for practical, corporate-friendly accommodation, breakfast, dinner on request, central booking, and easy access.

## Louvre Manor House
Quieter, more spacious, garden-led stay for guests wanting a more relaxed or refined setting.

## Little House
Private self-catering accommodation for independent travellers, small groups, longer stays, and guests who want privacy.

## The Gallery
Function and meeting venue for business functions, birthdays, kitchen teas, baby showers, funerals, memorial teas, and small conferences.

## WingerdHuis
To be defined. Possible role: heritage, group, private event, or overflow accommodation.

## Structure Rule
Each property page must answer:
- Who is this best for?
- What makes it different?
- What is included?
- How do I book?

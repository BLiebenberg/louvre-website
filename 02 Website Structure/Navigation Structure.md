# Navigation Structure

## Purpose
Make it easy for guests to choose the right Louvre offer and book quickly.

## Header Rules
- Keep the Louvre logo centered and clear.
- Keep menu labels short.
- Always include a visible booking or call action.
- Remove Shopify customer account, cart, and store-style links unless needed.

## Footer Rules
- Contact details.
- Addresses.
- Booking links.
- Google review link.
- Policies.
- Social links if active.

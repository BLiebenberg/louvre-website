# Colour Palette

## Purpose
Keep the website visually disciplined and recognisable.

## Primary Palette
- Paper / Clay: `#e9e6df`
- Charcoal: `#2f3030`

## Usage
- Paper / Clay: full site background, panels, cards, buttons where light.
- Charcoal: text, section lines, borders, dark buttons, icons.

## Rules
- Use paper/clay and charcoal as the default.
- Avoid extra beige, grey, lavender, or accent colours unless intentionally approved.
- Use photography for warmth instead of adding colour blocks.
- Dividers should be 1px charcoal.

## Button Treatment
- Primary: charcoal background, paper/clay text.
- Secondary: paper/clay background, charcoal border and text.

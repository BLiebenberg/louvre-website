# Brand Identity

## Purpose
Define Louvre as one trusted hospitality collection in Bothaville, not a set of disconnected properties.

## Core Brand Idea
Louvre is a calm, capable hospitality home base for guests who need accommodation, functions, food, and practical booking support handled properly.

## Brand Architecture
- Louvre
- Louvre Guesthouse
- Louvre Manor House
- Little House
- The Gallery
- WingerdHuis

## Positioning
One Louvre system. Multiple settings. One reliable standard.

## Proof Points
- Established since 1998.
- 27 en-suite rooms across two main properties.
- Central booking support.
- Function venue and catering capability.
- Strong fit for business, NAMPO, long stays, and family visits.

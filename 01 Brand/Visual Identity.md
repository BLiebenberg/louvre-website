# Visual Identity

## Purpose
Define the visual feel of the Louvre website and supporting marketing material.

## Direction
Clean country hospitality with modern business clarity.

## Visual Character
- Paper background.
- Charcoal typography and lines.
- Minimal colour.
- Strong real photography.
- Thin dividers.
- Calm spacing.
- No decorative clutter.

## Layout Principles
- Let the logo breathe.
- Use content sections divided by 1px charcoal lines.
- Use photography as proof, not decoration.
- Keep cards restrained and rectangular.
- Avoid rounded, glossy, or colourful interface elements.

## Imagery Role
Images must show the actual rooms, food, venue, gardens, guests, and atmosphere.

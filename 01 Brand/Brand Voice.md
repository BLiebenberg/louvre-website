# Brand Voice

## Purpose
Keep all website copy calm, confident, direct, and hospitable.

## Voice Principles
- Professional, but not cold.
- Elegant, but not precious.
- Helpful, but not over-explaining.
- Local, established, and trustworthy.
- Clear about booking steps.

## Tone
Use short, confident sentences. Avoid hype, jokes, and generic travel cliches.

## Good Phrases
- We take care of you while you take care of business.
- Choose the setting. Keep the same Louvre support.
- Rooms, functions, food, and booking support under one trusted name.
- Stay, meet, and gather in Bothaville.

## Avoid
- Luxury claims that feel inflated.
- Too much purple language.
- Overloaded paragraphs.
- Generic phrases like "unforgettable experience" unless made specific.

## Copy Checklist
- Is the booking action clear?
- Does the copy explain who the page is for?
- Does it sound like Louvre, not a template?
- Is the promise practical and believable?

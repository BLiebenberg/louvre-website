# Design Principles

## Purpose
Keep the Framer build visually disciplined.

## Principles
- Paper/clay and charcoal only.
- 1px charcoal section dividers.
- Real photography.
- Clear hierarchy.
- Generous but controlled spacing.
- Simple rectangular buttons.
- No decorative colour.
- No cards inside cards.
- No store-like interface elements.

## Layout
- Logo centered at top.
- Navigation below or beside logo depending viewport.
- Strong first screen with booking choices.
- Sections should feel editorial and calm.
- Use repeated layouts for clarity.

## Conversion
- Every major page needs an obvious CTA.
- Quote flows must be easy.
- Contact details should never be hard to find.

## Mobile
- Mobile is primary.
- Buttons must stack cleanly.
- Images must crop intentionally.

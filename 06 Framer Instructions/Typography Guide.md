# Typography Guide

## Purpose
Set a clean Louvre typography system.

## Font Direction
Use Calibri or a close clean sans-serif equivalent.

## Hierarchy
- H1: strong but not oversized.
- H2: clear section headings.
- H3: card and subsection titles.
- Body: readable and calm.
- Eyebrows: small uppercase labels.

## Rules
- Letter spacing should be 0.
- Avoid huge marketing hero text.
- Keep lines readable.
- Keep paragraph length short.
- Use bold sparingly.

## Suggested Sizes
- H1 desktop: 42 to 56px.
- H1 mobile: 34 to 40px.
- H2 desktop: 30 to 38px.
- H2 mobile: 26 to 32px.
- Body: 16 to 18px.
- Eyebrow: 12px.
